üëÆ Adding New Guards

You only need to do this once when a new guard joins.

Step 1: Open Menu

Click the ‚ûï Add Guard button on the right side.

Step 2: Enter Details

Type the Guard's Name in the text box.

Step 3: Take 4 Photos

Ask the guard to stand in front of the camera. The system needs to see their face from different angles:

  1. Front: Looking straight at the camera.
  2. Left: Turn head slightly left.
  3. Right: Turn head slightly right.
  4. Back: Turn around (optional).

‚ùì Easy Fixes (Troubleshooting)

Something not working? Try these simple solutions before calling IT.

Problem Solution
Screen is Black / No Video 1. Check if the camera USB wire is plugged in tight.
2. Click the Camera ON/OFF button again.
3. Restart the computer.
No Sound on Alert 1. Check if computer speakers are not muted.
2. Turn up the volume.
3. Make sure speakers are plugged in.
Guard is there, but says "Missing" 1. The room might be too dark. Turn on lights or use Night Mode.
2. The guard might be hiding their face. Ask them to look up.
System is very slow 1. Close other apps like YouTube or Chrome.
2. Click Stop Monitoring and start again.
